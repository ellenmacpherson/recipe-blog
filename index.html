Recipes for anxious times.

There are very few silver linings to COVID-19, but there has been one for me: spending time in my kitchen. I’ve always loved cooking, but in the past year it’s largely been sidelined because of poor mental health. I’ve neglected my stove and oven in favour of the microwave and takeaways. You’d think that a global pandemic would only make that worse, but it’s forced me to consider what I might cook if I was in lockdown for a few weeks. That train of thought led me here: to a blog of all the recipes I've cooked in self-isolation.

These are simple recipes to get you through the tough times. I hope they’ll also help you rediscover a love of home cooking.
